Wild Arugula Salad with Shaved Parmesan Garlic Croutons and Lemon

Serves 8

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Rub crust of bread with garlic clove. Tear bread into 1-inch pieces and place in large bowl toss with ¼ cup olive oil.
  2. Sprinkle bread lightly with salt; arrange in single layer on rimmed baking sheet. Bake until golden and crisp around edges, about 10 minutes. Cool.
  3. Place arugula in large bowl. Drizzle with 3 tablespoons olive oil and 2 tablespoons lemon juice, adding more lemon juice, if desired. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Add ²/3 of croutons and half of Parmesan cheese and toss. Transfer to serving bowl; scatter remaining croutons and Parmesan cheese over and serve.

What you need

  • 2 Large Bowls
  • Baking Sheet
  • Serving Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 8-ounce piece ciabatta with crust (preferably day-old)
  • 1 large garlic clove, peeled
  • ¼ cup plus 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 ounces arugula (about 10 cups)
  • 2 tablespoons (or more) fresh lemon juice
  • 3 ounces Parmesan cheese, shaved into strips
